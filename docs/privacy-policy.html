Privacy Policy

Carajás Field Guide · Effective date: March 2026

Overview

Carajás Field Guide is published by Mantella. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to being transparent and keeping your data minimal.

Information we collect

The app stores the following data locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party:

  • Seen species list — the species you have marked as seen, stored in the app's local storage (UserDefaults). This data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Language preference — whether you have enabled Brazilian Portuguese names, stored locally in UserDefaults.
  • Downloaded images — species photographs downloaded from iNaturalist on first launch, stored in your device's Documents directory for offline use. Images are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage data, crash reports, or analytics (at this time)

Future analytics

We may introduce anonymous usage analytics in a future version of the app to help us understand how the app is used and improve it. If and when analytics are added, this policy will be updated and users will be informed before the update is installed. Any analytics data collected will be anonymous and will not be linked to your identity.

Third-party services

The app loads species photographs from iNaturalist on first launch. These images are fetched directly from iNaturalist's servers. Your request to download these images may be visible to iNaturalist in accordance with their own privacy policy. Once downloaded, images are stored locally and no further requests are made.

The app does not use any advertising networks, social media SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no social features, and no in-app purchases.

Data retention and deletion

All data stored by the app lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. The seen species list and language preference can also be reset by clearing the app's data in iOS Settings.

Your rights

Because this app does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no data held externally to request, correct, or delete. All data is under your direct control on your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time, particularly if analytics or new features are introduced. The current version will always be available at this URL and within the app. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the app, please contact us at: hello@mantella.app
